Facts About Chamels

  • Camels' humps store up to 80lbs of fat, and they can live off of it for months.
  • Camels have 2 rows of eyelashs, which help protect their eyes from desert dust.
  • Camels can run up to 40mph.

Facts About Chameleons

  • There are about 160 different species of chameleons
  • Chameleons can see in visible and ultraviolet light.
  • Chameleons have a 360 degree vision around its body.

Facts About Pigeons

  • Pigeons are spiritual for some religions
  • The pigeon has about 10,000 feathers on its body.
  • Pigeons can find their way back to its nest which is 1300 miles away.

Facts About Pandas

  • Bamboo is 90% of a panda's diet.
  • Pandas have 6 fingers on each paw.
  • Pandas have huge growth spurts.
